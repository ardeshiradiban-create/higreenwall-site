FAQ

Quick answers clients ask in Toronto before they move forward.

How much does a living green wall cost?

It depends on size, system type, plant selection, and access. Send dimensions or photos and we’ll quote quickly.

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly and seasonal plans: trimming, nutrients, irrigation checks, pests prevention, and system tuning.

Can you do outdoor systems in Toronto winter?

Yes, with the right design and seasonal plan. Outdoor irrigation needs winterization and spring startup.

How fast can you start?

We can assess quickly. Install timing depends on scope and materials. Message us and we’ll give a realistic timeline.
