FAQ

Common questions about living green walls and irrigation projects in Toronto.

How much maintenance does a living green wall need?

It depends on the system, plant mix, and location. We design for low maintenance and can offer seasonal care. For indoor green walls, most owners prefer a simple scheduled plan.

Can you install in condos and commercial buildings?

Yes. We coordinate with property management for access, timing, and building requirements.

Do you offer smart controllers and sensors for irrigation?

Yes. We can integrate smart scheduling and sensor logic based on your site and planting needs.

Do you work outside Toronto?

Yes—across the GTA and Ontario depending on project scope.
